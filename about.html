About Us

At Penny Juice, we believe that healthy choices for kids should be colorful, tasty, and fun! We specialize in vibrant, fruit-flavored juice concentrates made from 100% fruit juice with no added sugar, no artificial preservatives, and absolutely no nonsense. Our mission? To keep children hydrated, energized, and happy—one delicious cup at a time.

Whether you're a daycare center, school, or organization serving young ones, Penny Juice is the smart, affordable solution for large-scale, kid-friendly nutrition. With six exciting color/flavor combos, easy-mix concentrates, and unbeatable value, we make it easy to serve healthy drinks kids actually love.

We're not just juice—we're Penny Juice. Bold in flavor. Bright in color. Better for kids.
